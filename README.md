# Sales and Profit DashBoard

**Project Overview**

The Sales & Profit Dashboard is an Excel-based interactive dashboard designed to analyze business sales performance, profitability, and customer insights.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key metrics such as total sales, total profit, yearly trends, category-wise performance, customer count, and monthly sales distribution.

**Objectives**

1. Track overall sales and profit performance

2. Compare profit across different years

3. Analyze category-wise and sub-category sales

4. Identify top customers by profit

5. Monitor monthly sales trends

6. Understand customer distribution
